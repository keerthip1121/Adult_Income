US Adult Income Prediction

Problem Statement:

To predict whether Income of a person lies in the category of "<=50K" or ">50k" by training the model with different Machine Learning classification algorithms using the given columns of information.

About Dataset:

Adult Income dataset was extracted by Barry Becker from 1994 US Census Database.This dataset consists of anonymous information such as age, occupation, education, race, work class, native_country, capital gain and loss and census income of two categories i.e, ">50K" or "<=50K".

End Goal:

Get the best algorithm model that predicts the Annual Income the best and Save that Model for future Deployement.
